Unit Conversion Made Simple

Convertor is an easy to use PHP unit conversion library.

Simply include the library in your project and you're away! Convert from one unit to another in seconds!

$demoConvertor = new Convertor(10, "m");
$demoConvertor->to("ft"); //convert meters to feet
